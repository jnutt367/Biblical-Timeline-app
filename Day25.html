"Give, and it will be given to you..."

– Luke 6:38

Welcome to Day 25 – The Blessing of Generosity

Generosity is more than money — it’s giving time, love, and encouragement. God calls us to give freely.

God promises blessings 'pressed down, shaken together, and running over.' Our generosity sows seeds that grow beyond measure.

Generosity reflects God’s heart. Giving with joy mirrors Christ’s love and opens us to His favor.

“A generous person will prosper; whoever refreshes others will be refreshed.” – Proverbs 11:25

“Whoever sows generously will also reap generously.” – 2 Corinthians 9:6-7

“Bring the whole tithe… and see if I will not throw open the floodgates of heaven.” – Malachi 3:10

Consider one way to give today — a kind word, a small gift, or your time. Give expecting nothing, trusting God to multiply it.

Lord, teach us to give with cheerful hearts. May our generosity reflect Your love and bring hope, provision, and joy to those around us. Amen.
