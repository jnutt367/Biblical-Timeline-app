Week 3 — Identity, Freedom & Renewal

Scripture: Acts 17:11, Hebrews 4:12

Back to Overview Next: Week 4

Lesson

God wants you to know who you are in Him. By studying Scripture, you can discover your identity as His child and experience freedom from fear, doubt, and sin. Take time to explore the context of passages, note repeated themes, and cross-reference verses that speak to your life.

Engaging deeply with Scripture strengthens your mind and spirit. Ask questions, highlight words, and make notes. Using study tools, such as commentaries, devotionals, or your course apps, can help you understand God’s message more fully.

Read: Acts 17:11

Tip: Compare translations — what words change the most?

Assignments

  • Choose one passage to study deeply this week.
  • Create a mind map of key themes and insights.
  • Share your discoveries in the Teams community.

Reflection Questions

  • What new insight did I discover?
  • How does this passage relate to my life?
  • What verse will I memorize this week?
