Week 1 — Falling in Love With Scripture

Scripture: Psalm 119:105, John 1:1-5, Romans 12:1-2

Lesson

The Bible is God’s living Word, active and powerful (Hebrews 4:12). It is not just a book of rules or stories — it is God speaking to you personally. Scripture lights your path (Psalm 119:105) and guides your thoughts, actions, and decisions.

Reading the Bible daily helps you know God, understand His will, and grow spiritually. Begin simply: read a few verses, reflect on them, and ask God to reveal His truth. Journaling your thoughts can deepen your understanding and help you remember what God has spoken.

Assignments

  • Read the daily passages listed above.
  • Post your Day 1 reflection in the Teams community (#daily-scripture).
  • Set a consistent reading time and place for your devotional time.

Reflection Questions

  • What stood out to me from today’s reading?
  • How can I apply this verse today?
  • What questions do I have for God about this passage?
