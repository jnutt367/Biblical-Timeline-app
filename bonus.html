Bonus — Spiritual Warfare

Scripture: Ephesians 6:10-18, 2 Corinthians 10:4-5

Back to Overview

Lesson

God equips you for spiritual battle through His Word. The Armor of God protects your mind, heart, and spirit. Scripture declares that our struggle is not against people but spiritual forces (Ephesians 6:12).

Daily declarations, prayers, and meditating on Scripture strengthen your defenses. Stand firm, put on God’s armor, and rely on His Word to overcome fear, doubt, and spiritual attacks.

Read: Ephesians 6:10-18

Tip: Pray each morning and declare one truth aloud.

Assignments

  • Pray over your home daily for seven days.
  • Declare Scripture out loud each morning.
  • Use Jesus Love Revealed apps for guided study and prayer.

Reflection Questions

  • Which part of the armor of God will I focus on today?
  • What prayer or declaration strengthens me?
  • How will I use apps or tools to grow closer to God?
