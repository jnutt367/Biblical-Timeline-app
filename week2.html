Week 2 — Prayer & Hearing God

Scripture: Joshua 1:8, 2 Timothy 3:16-17

Lesson

Prayer is more than words; it is a conversation with God. When you read the Bible, invite God to speak through His Word. Ask the Holy Spirit to guide your understanding and show you how to apply the Scriptures to your life.

A simple 3-step Bible reading method is:

  1. Read – Focus on a short passage.
  2. Reflect – Ask “What is God saying to me?”
  3. Respond – Write a prayer, a thought, or an action step.

Consistency matters. Choose a translation that’s easy to understand and set aside a time each day to read and pray. Use your Jesus Love Revealed apps as tools to guide your learning and reflection.

Assignments

  • Pray before reading daily.
  • Use the 3-step method on one passage each day.
  • Journal insights in your reflection notebook.

Reflection Questions

  • What did God teach me today?
  • Which step of the 3-step method was most helpful?
  • What habit will I focus on this week?
