Week 4 — Purpose, Calling & Obedience

Scripture: James 1:22-25, Colossians 3:16-17

Back to Overview Next: Bonus

Lesson

Reading and reflecting on Scripture is not enough without action. God calls you to obey His Word and live it out. Discover your purpose by applying lessons learned, serving others, and growing in faith.

Maintaining consistency over time helps your heart remain open to God. Write down practical steps for obedience, reflect on victories and struggles, and encourage others to grow with you. Your daily walk with God is meant to influence every part of life — your work, relationships, and spiritual growth.

Read: James 1:22

Tip: Turn one insight into a 7-day action plan.

Assignments

  • Write a 7-day plan to obey one Scripture principle daily.
  • Share your mission statement in Teams.
  • Invite one person to join the course.

Reflection Questions

  • What action step will I take this week?
  • Which verse will guide me daily?
  • How can I encourage someone else this week?
